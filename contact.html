Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

What areas in Namibia do you service?

We provide transportation and logistics services across all 14 regions of Namibia, with special coverage in Windhoek, Walvis Bay, Swakopmund, and other major urban centers.

How quickly can you dispatch a vehicle?

For standard requests, we can dispatch vehicles within 2-4 hours. Emergency and priority requests can be fulfilled within 1 hour in most urban areas. Response times may vary based on location and vehicle availability.

What types of cargo do you transport?

We transport a wide range of cargo including general merchandise, construction materials, industrial equipment, perishable goods (with refrigerated options), and specialized cargo. Certain hazardous materials require special arrangements.

How do you ensure cargo safety?

All our vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking, temperature monitoring (where applicable), and secure locking systems. Our drivers are trained in cargo securing techniques, and we carry comprehensive insurance for all shipments.

Do you offer tracking for shipments?

Yes, we provide real-time GPS tracking for all shipments. Customers receive a tracking link upon dispatch and can monitor their cargo's progress through our client portal or mobile app.
