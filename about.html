Our Story

Revolutionizing logistics in Namibia through technology, trust, and transparency.

Connecting Namibia

BaTLorriH was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: to make logistics in Namibia more accessible, reliable, and efficient. We saw the challenges businesses and individuals faced in finding reliable transport for their goods.

What started as a solution for small businesses in Windhoek has grown into Namibia's leading digital logistics platform, connecting shippers with verified transport providers across the country.

Today, we're proud to support hundreds of businesses and thousands of drivers, moving everything from household goods to industrial equipment across Namibia's diverse landscapes.
